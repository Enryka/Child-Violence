Reasons for Reform

Mistaken Witness Identification

Over ONE QUARTER of exonerations are due to mistaken identification

Perjury or False Accusation

In 62% of exonerations, the innocent have been falsely accused, or a victim of perjury

False Confessions

While false confessions have been involved in 12% of all known exonerations, it makes up 28% of DNA-based exonerations

False or Misleading Forensic Evidence

Roughly 23% of exonerations have involved false or misleading forensic science

Official Misconduct

Official and police misconduct has contributed to 57% of known exonerations

Inadequate Legal Defense

Over ONE QUARTER of exonerees had an inadequate legal defense
